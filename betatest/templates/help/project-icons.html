Project icons

This icon is displayed in every list your project appears in.

Default icons

The default icon for your project is generated as an identicon by the online avatar service gravatar.com (which is also used to fetch the user-icons). The project title is included in this generation process, so changing your project title might also change your default icon.

Upload a custom icon

{{ helpImageStatic('about/img/project-sidebar.png', 'The project page sidebar.') }} It is possible to upload a custom icon for your project. You will find a link for this in your project page sidebar.

You will find a form displaying your current icon. On the right you are able to select an image file from your computer. This file must meet the following guidelines in order to be a valid icon. Otherwise the upload will fail.

  • Icons have to be in PNG, JPG or GIF format.
  • Icons should be at least 128 x 128 pixels in size, but may not exceed 512 x 512 pixels.
  • If your icon is too small, it will be scaled up. This will reduce its quality.
  • Your icon may be max. 250 KB in size. If you cannot fit this, choose another size or compression. We suggest PNG.
  • Your icon must meet the content guidelines.
  • You have to own the right to publish the icon without violating any copyright.

Tips for a good icon

It can be difficult to find a good icon for your project. You always need to keep in mind that this is what other users see first, and that they recognize your project by its icon. So follow these few steps:

  1. Don't use a complicated icon. In some lists the icon will be scaled down to 16x16 pixels. If your icon is too complicated, one cannot identify it well.
  2. Have contrast. Your icon will be better recognizale with a high contrast against its background.
  3. Capture the main characteristic of your project. Otherwise one might not remember what your project was about when seeing the icon.

You might also want to google for icon design tips, to create an icon that fits your project.
